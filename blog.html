Problem 1

Let \((X_n)\) be a sequence of random variables. We say that

  • \((X_n)\) converges in probability to \(X\) if, for all \(\epsilon >0\)
    \[\lim_n \mathbb{P}(|X_n-X|>\epsilon)=0\]
  • \((X_n)\) converges to \(X\) almost surely if,
    \[\mathbb{P}\left(\{\lim_n X_n\ne X\}\right)=0\]
  • \((X_n)\) converges to \(X\) in \(L^p(\mathbb{P})\) (for \(p\ge 1\)) if,
    \[\lim_n\mathbb{E}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]=0\]
  • \((X_n)\) converges to \(X\) monotonically if \((X_n)\) is either increasing or decreasing to \(X\) a.s.

Now prove that all the convergence above imply the convergence in probability.

Solution:

First we show that

By the definition of almost surely convergence, we have
\[\mathbb{P}\left(\{\lim_n X_n\ne X\}\right)=0\]
which can be rephrased as, \((X_n)\) converges to \(X\) almost surely, for any \(\epsilon>0,\exists N \ni \forall n>N\),
\[\mathbb{P}\left(|X_n-X|>\epsilon\right)=0;\implies \lim_n \mathbb{P}\left(|X_n-X|>\epsilon\right)=0\]
Therefore,

Next, we want to show

Since \((X_n)\) converges to \(X\) in \(L^p\) (for \(p>1\)) we have, for any \(\epsilon>0\),

\[\lim_n \mathbb{E}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]=0;\implies \frac{\lim_n \mathbb{E}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]}{\epsilon^n}=0;\hspace{2mm} \implies \lim_n \frac{\mathbb{E}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]}{\epsilon^n}=0\]
By the Chebyshev’s inequality we have, for any \(\epsilon>0\), if \(X\) is integrable then,
\[\begin{align*} \mathbb{P}\left(|X_n|>\epsilon\right)&\le \frac{\mathbb{E}\left[|X_n|\right]}{\epsilon} \end{align*}\]
Thus, we have
\[\lim_n \frac{\mathbb{E}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]}{\epsilon^n}\ge \lim_n \mathbb{P}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]=0;\hspace{2mm}\implies \lim_n \mathbb{P}\left[|X_n-X|^p\right]=0\]
Lastly, we want to show that

Let \((X_n)\) converges to a finite limit \(X\) increasingly and almost surely. Then for any \(\epsilon>0,\exists N \ni \forall n>N\) we have, \(X_n<X+\epsilon\).
Then, for all \(n>N\),
\[\mathbb{P}\left(|X_n-X|\right)=\mathbb{P}\left(X_n-X>\epsilon\right)=\mathbb{P}\left(X_n>X+\epsilon\right)=0\implies \lim_n \mathbb{P}\left(|X_n-X|\right)=0\]
Similar arguments can be drawn for monotonically decreasing sequences.
